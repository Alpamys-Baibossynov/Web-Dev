Fluffy Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredient Amount
Flour 1.5 cups
Milk 1.25 cups

Step 1: Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.

Step 2: Add milk and stir until smooth.

Step 3: Cook on a hot pan until golden.

Garlic Butter Pasta

  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredient Amount
Pasta 200 g
Butter 3 tbsp

Step 1: Boil pasta until tender.

Step 2: Melt butter and add garlic.

Step 3: Toss pasta with sauce.

Fruit Smoothie

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredient Amount
Banana 1
Milk 1 cup

Step 1: Add all ingredients to a blender.

Step 2: Blend until smooth.

Step 3: Pour and enjoy.
