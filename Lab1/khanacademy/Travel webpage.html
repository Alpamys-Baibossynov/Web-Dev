Travel to the United States of America

  • Places to see in the USA
  • Top 5 states to visit

USA is one of the greatest countries in the world, especially if you enjoy pop culture.
You can visit places you have seen in movies and TV shows.
Each state has its own vibe.

Places to see:

  • Grand Canyon

    • One of the greatest canyons in the world, located in Arizona.

  • Niagara Falls

    • Famous waterfalls on the border between the USA and Canada.

  • Mount Rushmore

    • Monument with faces of four US presidents.

  • Las Vegas

    • The city of entertainment and bright lights.

Top 5 states to visit:

  1. California
  2. Nevada
  3. Illinois
  4. Hawaii
  5. Florida
